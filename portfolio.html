Academic Research and Project

While pursuing Bachelor of Technology from SNDT Women's University (2012-2016), I worked on a research project as part of my curriculum.

Title: Medical Emergency System for Road Accidents using Airbot (Quadcopter)

Summary: The project focuses on developing a system that caters to the need of medical emergencies that arise during road accidents. This was motivated by an initial analysis with the help of the RTO of the number of accident victims suffering due to not receiving timely medical help. In this project, an Airbot is used to deliver the required first aid. Various means of communication are used to achieve this goal such as the use of GPS, along with the use of an integrated camera to help view the accident site.

Publication: A technical paper on “Design and control of electronics based medical emergency system using a Quadcopter” was published in ‘International Journal on Recent and innovation trends in computing and communication (IJRITCC) May 16 Volume 4 Issue 5’.
